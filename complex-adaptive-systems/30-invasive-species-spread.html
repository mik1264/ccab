Controls

Local Lotka-Volterra-style competition. An occupied cell may die at mortality rate; empty cells receive colonists from neighbors within dispersal range, weighted by the colonist's effective fecundity (r × competitive weight). Invaders have a multiplicative edge in the competition lottery.

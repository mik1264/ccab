Qubit Superposition

A qubit can exist in a superposition of |0⟩ and |1⟩ states. This is visualized on the Bloch sphere, where any point represents a valid qubit state.

Try applying quantum gates:
X: Bit flip (NOT gate)
H: Hadamard (creates superposition)
Z: Phase flip
S, T: Phase rotations

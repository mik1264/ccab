Quantum Harmonic Oscillator

Unlike classical oscillators that can have any energy, quantum oscillators can only have discrete energy levels: En = (n + ½)ℏω

Key features:
• Energy is quantized (comes in discrete packets)
• Ground state (n=0) has non-zero energy (zero-point energy)
• Higher levels have more nodes (zeros) in the wave function
• Probability spreads wider at higher energies

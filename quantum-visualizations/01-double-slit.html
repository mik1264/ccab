Double-Slit Experiment

Particles: 0
Mode: Wave-like

What You're Seeing

This is the famous double-slit experiment. Particles are fired one at a time at a barrier with two slits. Even though each particle goes through individually, they build up an interference pattern as if they were waves!

Click "Toggle Observation" to see what happens when we try to observe which slit the particle goes through. The interference pattern disappears! This is quantum measurement in action.

Aeroelastic Flutter

The Tacoma Narrows Bridge failed due to aeroelastic flutter — a self-excited torsional oscillation, NOT simple resonance.

(Tacoma Narrows: ratio ~72, modern bridges: ~30)

Stable
Above the critical flutter speed (~35 mph for Tacoma Narrows), aerodynamic forces couple with torsion to create negative damping — oscillations grow without bound until structural failure.

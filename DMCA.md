# DMCA Policy

## Notification of Infringement

If you believe that content on CCAB infringes your copyright, please send a DMCA takedown notice containing:

1. **Your signature** (physical or electronic)
2. **Identification of the copyrighted work** claimed to be infringed
3. **Identification of the infringing material** with sufficient detail to locate it (e.g., URL)
4. **Your contact information** (address, phone number, email)
5. **A statement** that you have a good faith belief that use of the material is not authorized
6. **A statement** that the information in the notification is accurate, under penalty of perjury

## Where to Send Notices

DMCA notices should be submitted as a GitHub issue with the label "dmca" or via the repository's issue tracker.

## Counter-Notification

If you believe your content was removed in error, you may submit a counter-notification containing:

1. **Your signature**
2. **Identification of the removed material** and its location before removal
3. **A statement under penalty of perjury** that you have a good faith belief the material was removed by mistake
4. **Your name, address, and phone number**
5. **Consent to jurisdiction** in your local federal district court

## Response Timeline

- We will respond to valid DMCA notices within 48-72 hours
- Content will be removed or disabled pending resolution
- Counter-notifications will be processed within 10-14 business days

## Repeat Infringers

Users who repeatedly infringe copyrights may be banned from contributing to the project.

## Good Faith

We respond to DMCA notices in good faith. Misrepresentation in a DMCA notice may result in liability for damages.

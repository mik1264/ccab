‚ùå Confirmation Bias

We instinctively look for evidence that CONFIRMS the rule (A and 4). But to TEST a rule, we must look for what could FALSIFY it.

If P‚ÜíQ, then NOT-Q ‚Üí NOT-P. If odd (not-Q) has vowel (P), rule fails. Most people don't think to check the "7" card.

Philosophical Perspectives

üîó Spatiotemporal Continuity

üß© Material Constitution

Identity is in the physical stuff. Ship B (made from original planks) is the true ship because it contains Theseus' actual materials.

üìã Pattern Identity

What matters is the pattern/structure, not the specific atoms. Both ships have valid claims‚Äîidentity isn't strictly about matter or history.

üö´ No-Self View

üéØ Why It's Inescapable

The law exploits our cognitive blind spots: we underestimate unknown unknowns, forget past failures, and confuse best-case scenarios with expected outcomes. Even when we know we'll underestimate, we underestimate how much we'll underestimate. The self-reference isn't a bug‚Äîit's the point. No finite correction can capture infinite regress.

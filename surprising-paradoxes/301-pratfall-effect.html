The Pratfall Effect

Why Competent People Become MORE Likable After Blundering

Aronson et al. (1966) discovered something counterintuitive: when a highly competent person makes a clumsy mistake, they become MORE attractive, not less!

But when an average person makes the same mistake? They become LESS likable. The blunder only helps if you're already impressive.

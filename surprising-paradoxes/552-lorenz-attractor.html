Weather Prediction

Lorenz discovered this while modeling atmospheric convection on a computer. Rounding an initial value from 0.506127 to 0.506 produced completely different weather predictions‚Äîa 0.0001% difference!

Deterministic Chaos

The system is completely deterministic‚Äîno randomness involved. Yet long-term prediction is impossible because tiny measurement errors grow exponentially. The butterfly-wing shape never repeats exactly.

Strange Attractor

Despite being unpredictable, all trajectories are "attracted" to this butterfly shape. The attractor has fractal dimension ~2.06, meaning it's more than a surface but less than a solid.

The Butterfly Effect

Lorenz's 1972 talk "Does the Flap of a Butterfly's Wings in Brazil Set Off a Tornado in Texas?" gave chaos theory its most famous metaphor. Small causes can have huge, unpredictable effects.

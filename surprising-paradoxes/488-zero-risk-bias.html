Cognitive Closure

A residual risk of 0.1% still feels like "risk exists." Zero means the problem is truly solved‚Äîno more mental burden, no more worry. The brain craves closure.

Proportional Thinking

We think in ratios, not differences. 5%‚Üí0% is a 100% reduction! 55%‚Üí50% is only a 9% reduction. The math is the same, but the framing is completely different.

Policy Implications

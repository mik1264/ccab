The Setup

This paradox, discovered by Swedish mathematician Lennart Ekbom in 1943, was inspired by a real announcement: the Swedish government declared a civil defense drill would occur during the following week, but the exact day would be a surprise.

"I shall be hanged at noon on a day next week, but I won't know which day until the executioner arrives. Let me reason through this carefully..."

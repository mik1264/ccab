üí° THE KEY INSIGHT

The 1 lb of solids never changes! At 99% water, 1 lb is 1% of 100 lbs. At 98% water, that same 1 lb must be 2% of the total‚Äîso the total must be 50 lbs!

üßÆ The Algebra

Let's work through it step by step:

Final: 1 lb solid = 2% of total

The "trick" is that going from 99% to 98% water means the solid percentage doubles from 1% to 2%. For the same mass to double its percentage, the total must halve!

üé≠ Why It Fools Us

Our intuition fails because:

  • We focus on the "small" 1% water change
  • We ignore that the solid percentage doubles
  • We think in terms of water lost, not ratios
  • The problem seems too simple to require careful math

This is a veridical paradox: the math is correct, but the result feels wrong!

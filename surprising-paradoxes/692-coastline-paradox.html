Why Does Length Increase?

üìú Historical Development

1967 - Benoit Mandelbrot

1975 - Fractal Geometry
Mandelbrot develops fractal geometry, explaining why coastlines (and many natural phenomena) have non-integer dimensions between 1 and 2.

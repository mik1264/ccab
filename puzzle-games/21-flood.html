How to Play Flood-It

The goal is to make the entire board one single color in as few moves as possible.

Starting from the top-left corner, click a color button below the grid to flood-fill the connected region with that color.

Each turn, the flooded region expands to absorb all adjacent cells of the chosen color.

Par is the target number of moves. Try to finish at or under par!

Difficulty: Adjust grid size and number of colors using the dropdowns.

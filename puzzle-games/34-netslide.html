How to Play

  • Each tile has pipe segments connecting to its edges (up, down, left, right).
  • The center tile is the source (marked with a dot).
  • Use the arrows at the edges to slide entire rows or columns. Tiles wrap around.
  • Goal: connect every pipe tile to the source so the whole network is linked.
  • Connected pipes glow green; disconnected ones stay grey.

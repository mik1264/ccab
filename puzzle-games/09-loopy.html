How to Play Loopy

Draw edges between dots to form a single closed loop.

Rules:

1. Numbers inside cells indicate how many of that cell's 4 edges are part of the loop.

2. The loop must be a single closed path - no branches or dead ends.

3. Not every cell needs a number clue.

Controls: Click on an edge (between two adjacent dots) to toggle it on/off.

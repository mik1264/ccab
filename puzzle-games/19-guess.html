Mastermind

Guess the hidden sequence of colored pegs

0:00

How to Play

A secret sequence of colored pegs is hidden. You have a limited number of guesses to figure it out.

Select a color from the palette below, then click a peg in the current row to place it. Fill all positions, then click Submit.

Feedback pegs:

● Black = correct color in correct position

○ White = correct color but wrong position

Empty = that peg's color is not in the remaining unmatched positions.

How to Play

  • The grid contains numbered tiles that need to be arranged in order (1, 2, 3...).
  • Click the arrows at the edges to slide an entire row or column.
  • Tiles wrap around when pushed off the edge.
  • Arrange all tiles in order from left-to-right, top-to-bottom.

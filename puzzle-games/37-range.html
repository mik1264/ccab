How to Play Range

Place black squares in the grid. Each numbered cell tells you the total number of white cells visible from it horizontally and vertically (including itself), before being blocked by a black cell or the grid edge.

Rules:

1. No two black cells can be adjacent orthogonally (horizontally or vertically).

2. All white cells must form a single connected group.

3. Numbered cells cannot be made black.

Click a cell to toggle it black. Click again to clear it. You cannot click numbered cells.

How to Play Galaxies

Divide the grid into regions by drawing edges between cells.

Rules:

1. Each region must contain exactly one dot (galaxy center).

2. Each region must be 180-degree rotationally symmetric around its dot.

3. Every cell belongs to exactly one region.

Controls: Click on the border between two cells to toggle a wall. Walls separate regions.

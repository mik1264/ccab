How to Play

Draw a single closed loop that passes through every pearl on the grid.

Black pearls (●): The loop must turn 90° on the pearl AND go straight through both adjacent cells.

White pearls (○): The loop must go straight through the pearl AND turn 90° on at least one adjacent cell in the line.

Click on edges between cells to toggle them as part of the loop. The loop must form one single closed path.

Peg Solitaire

Jump pegs to remove them — leave exactly one!

How to Play

The goal is to remove pegs until only one remains.

A peg can jump over an adjacent peg (horizontally or vertically) into an empty hole beyond it. The jumped peg is removed.

Click a peg to select it (it highlights). Then click an empty hole two spaces away to jump. Valid destinations are shown in green.

The game ends when no more moves are possible. You win if exactly one peg remains!

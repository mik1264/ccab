How to Play Same Game

Click on a group of 2 or more adjacent same-colored squares to remove them.

When squares are removed, those above fall down. Empty columns collapse to the left.

Scoring: Removing n squares scores (n-2)² points. Bigger groups score much more!

Bonus: Clear the entire board for a large bonus.

Game Over: When no more groups of 2+ exist.

Towers

Fill the grid — clues tell how many towers are visible

Time: 0:00

Solved!

How to Play

  • Fill each cell with a number from 1 to N.
  • Each row and column must contain each number exactly once.
  • Think of each number as a tower of that height.
  • Clues on the edges tell you how many towers are visible from that direction.
  • A taller tower blocks all shorter towers behind it.
  • Example: the sequence 2,1,4,3 has 2 towers visible from the left (2 and 4).
  • Click a cell, then click a number (or use keyboard 1-6).

How to Play

Fill cells in the grid to reveal a hidden pattern. The numbers along the top and left edges are clues.

Each clue is a sequence of numbers indicating groups of consecutive filled cells in that row or column. Groups are separated by at least one empty cell.

Left-click (or tap) to fill a cell.
Right-click (or long-press) to mark a cell as empty (✕).
Click again to clear.

Complete the pattern to win!

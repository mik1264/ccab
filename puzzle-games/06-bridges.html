How to Play Bridges

Connect islands (numbered circles) with horizontal or vertical bridges.

Rules:

1. Each island's number shows how many bridges connect to it.

2. At most 2 bridges can connect any pair of adjacent islands.

3. Bridges cannot cross each other.

4. All islands must be connected into one group.

Controls: Click between two islands to add a bridge. Click again to add a second bridge. Click a third time to remove both.

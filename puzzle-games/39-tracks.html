How to Play Tracks

Draw a single continuous railway track from A to B.

Numbers along the top and left edges tell you how many cells in that column or row contain track.

Some track segments may already be given (shown in dark). You need to fill in the rest.

Click an empty cell to toggle track on/off. You cannot remove pre-placed track.

The track must form a single connected path from A to B, with no branches.

Undead

Place ghosts, vampires, and zombies in the grid

Time: 0:00

Solved!

How to Play

  • Place Vampires (V), Ghosts (G), and Zombies (Z) in empty cells.
  • Diagonal mirrors (\ and /) reflect your line of sight.
  • Vampires are visible only in direct line of sight (not reflected through mirrors).
  • Ghosts are visible only through mirrors (reflected path).
  • Zombies are always visible (direct or reflected).
  • Numbers on edges show how many monsters are visible from that direction.
  • Click empty cells to cycle: V → G → Z → empty.

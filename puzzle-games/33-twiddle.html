Twiddle

Rotate groups of tiles to order them

Moves: 0

Left-click: rotate clockwise • Right-click: rotate counter-clockwise

Solved!

How to Play

  • The grid has numbered tiles in a scrambled order.
  • Click at the intersection of 4 tiles to rotate those 4 tiles clockwise.
  • Right-click to rotate counter-clockwise.
  • A highlighted circle shows which intersection you are about to rotate.
  • Goal: arrange tiles in numerical order (1, 2, 3...) left-to-right, top-to-bottom.

How to Play

Goal: Place one tent orthogonally adjacent to each tree.

Rules:

1. Each tree has exactly one tent paired with it (up/down/left/right).

2. No two tents touch each other, not even diagonally.

3. The numbers at the edges show how many tents are in that row/column.

Controls: Click an empty cell to place a tent. Click again to mark as grass (no tent). Click again to clear.

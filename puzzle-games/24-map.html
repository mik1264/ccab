How to Play Four Color Map

Click on any region to cycle through the 4 available colors. Click again to change the color or remove it.

Your goal: color every region using at most 4 colors such that no two adjacent regions share the same color.

The four-color theorem guarantees this is always possible!

Conflicts shows how many pairs of adjacent regions have matching colors.

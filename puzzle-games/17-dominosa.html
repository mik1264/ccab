How to Play

A complete set of dominoes has been used to tile the rectangle. Your job is to figure out where each domino is placed.

Each domino (a,b) with a ≤ b appears exactly once. Click on the border between two adjacent cells to mark or unmark a domino edge.

When you correctly mark all domino boundaries around a pair, it forms a domino. Each pair must be used exactly once.

How to Play Slant

Fill every cell with a diagonal line (either \ or /).

Rules:

1. Numbers at intersections show how many diagonals meet at that point (0-4).

2. The diagonals must not form any closed loops.

Controls: Left-click to place \, click again for /, click again to clear.

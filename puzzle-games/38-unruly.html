How to Play Unruly

Fill every cell in the grid with either black or white.

Rules:

1. Each row and each column must contain equal numbers of black and white cells.

2. No three consecutive cells in any row or column may be the same color.

Click a cell to cycle: empty → black → white → empty.

Pre-filled cells (with a dot) cannot be changed.

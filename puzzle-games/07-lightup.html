How to Play Light Up

Place light bulbs on white cells to illuminate the entire grid.

Rules:

1. Bulbs illuminate all cells in their row and column until blocked by a wall.

2. No two bulbs may illuminate each other (no two in same row/column without a wall between).

3. Numbered walls show exactly how many bulbs must be adjacent (up/down/left/right).

4. All white cells must be illuminated.

Controls: Click a white cell to place/remove a bulb.

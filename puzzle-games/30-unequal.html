How to Play

Fill the grid with numbers 1 to N (where N is the grid size). Each row and column must contain each number exactly once (a Latin square).

The inequality signs (< and >) between cells must be satisfied by the numbers you place.

Click a cell to select it, then type a number or use the number pad below. Press Delete/Backspace to clear.

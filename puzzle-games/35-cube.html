How to Play

  • A cube sits on a grid with blue squares.
  • Roll the cube using arrow keys, the on-screen D-pad, or swipe.
  • When the cube rolls onto a blue square, the bottom face picks it up (turns that face blue).
  • But if the bottom face is already blue, rolling onto a blue square puts it back.
  • Collect all blue squares from the grid. The cube has 6 faces to store them.
  • Plan your route carefully -- face orientation matters!

Reynolds Number

Re = (density x velocity x chord) / viscosity. It describes the ratio of inertial to viscous forces. At low Re (tiny insects), the air feels thick and syrupy. At high Re (birds), inertia dominates. Insect flight spans Re 10 to 10,000, a regime where both viscous and inertial effects matter.

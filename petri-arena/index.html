Petri Dish Neural Cellular Automata (PD-NCA)
Welcome to Petri Arena! This gallery contains 20 interactive visualizations exploring the concepts from the Petri Dish Neural Cellular Automata research paper.

What is PD-NCA? Unlike traditional Neural Cellular Automata that create single morphologies, PD-NCA enables multiple NCA agents to compete and learn simultaneously in a shared 2D environment. Each agent adapts through backpropagation during the simulation, leading to open-ended emergent dynamics like cycles, cooperation, and spatial patterns.

üß¨ What is PD-NCA?

Petri Dish Neural Cellular Automata (PD-NCA) is a multi-agent system where multiple NCA "organisms" share a 2D grid and continuously adapt through backpropagation during the simulation‚Äînot just in pre-training. This creates open-ended, emergent dynamics including:

  • Cycles & Oscillations: Agents form dynamic patterns that evolve indefinitely
  • Cooperation & Competition: Attack/defense channels create territorial battles
  • Emergent Complexity: Learning produces rich behavior; no-learning leads to boring steady states
  • Spatial Patterns: Spirals, waves, and cycles emerge from simple local rules

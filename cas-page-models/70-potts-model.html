q-state Potts Model

Generalises Ising: each cell takes one of q states. Bond energy E = −J·Σδ(s_i, s_j). Metropolis updates drive domain coarsening at low T; for q≥5 the transition is first-order. Soap-bubble like grain growth when J>0.

Brusselator Model

dX/dt = A - (B+1)X + X²Y + Du∇²X

dY/dt = BX - X²Y + Dv∇²Y

A model for autocatalytic chemical reactions. When B > 1 + A², the system becomes unstable and forms Turing patterns through diffusion-driven instability.

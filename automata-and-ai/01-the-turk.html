The Mechanical Turk

The Mechanical Turk, 1770

Built by Wolfgang von Kempelen to impress Empress Maria Theresa of Austria, this chess-playing "automaton" defeated Napoleon Bonaparte and Benjamin Franklin.

For 84 years, audiences believed a machine could play chess. The truth? A skilled human operator hid inside the cabinet, moving pieces with magnets.

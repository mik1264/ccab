Lat = ZD + Dec = (90° - Ho) + Dec
The noon sight: measure the sun at its highest point to find your latitude. Watch the sun arc across the sky. At local noon, read the sextant angle. Subtract from 90° to get zenith distance, add the sun's declination: that's your latitude.

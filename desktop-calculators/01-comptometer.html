About the Comptometer

Patented by Dorr Felt in 1887, the Comptometer was the first commercially successful key-driven calculator. Unlike later machines, pressing a key directly adds that value — no crank or handle needed.

Skilled operators could press keys in multiple columns simultaneously, achieving speeds that rivaled early electronic calculators.

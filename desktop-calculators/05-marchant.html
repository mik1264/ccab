About the Marchant

The Marchant Calculator, manufactured in Oakland, California from 1911, was renowned for its speed and reliability. The “Silent Speed” model was famous for its smooth, quiet operation.

Marchant calculators featured proportional speed control — the operator could adjust how fast the automatic cycles ran. The full keyboard matrix allowed rapid number entry with one keypress per column.

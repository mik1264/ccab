About the Burroughs

William Seward Burroughs patented his adding machine in 1888. The key innovation was the printing mechanism — every addition was recorded on a paper tape, creating an audit trail that revolutionized bookkeeping.

The pull handle both powered the addition mechanism and advanced the paper tape. A special “complementary subtraction” method allowed the machine to subtract as well.

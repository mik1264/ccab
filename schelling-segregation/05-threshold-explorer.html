Understanding the Critical Threshold

Below 30%:
Integrated patterns remain stable. Small preference for similarity doesn't overcome random mixing.
30-35% (Critical):
Tipping point region. Small preference changes cause dramatic pattern changes. Very sensitive.
Above 35%:
Strong segregation emerges. Agents cluster tightly. Eventual similarity often exceeds 70-80%.

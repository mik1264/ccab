About Historical Simulations

Journey through time with interactive models that reveal the hidden patterns of human history. From the emergence of ancient trade networks to the rise and fall of civilizations, these simulations draw on archaeology, anthropology, and systems dynamics to visualize the forces that shaped our past. Explore how geography influenced settlement patterns, how writing evolved from pictographs to abstract symbols, and what factors lead societies to flourish or collapse.

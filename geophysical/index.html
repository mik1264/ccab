Geophysical Simulations

Explore Earth's dynamic processes through 10 interactive simulations of plate tectonics, erosion, weather systems, ocean currents, volcanic activity, glaciers, earthquakes, and climate cycles.

01

Plate Tectonics

Watch continental drift driven by mantle convection cells beneath tectonic plates. See continents collide, separate, and create mountain ranges.

 02

Erosion Simulation

Hydraulic erosion carves valleys and rivers into a 3D heightmap terrain. Water flows downhill, carrying sediment and reshaping the landscape.

 03

Weather Fronts

Cold and warm air masses interact to create pressure systems, fronts, and precipitation patterns. Watch weather systems evolve in real-time.

 04

Ocean Currents

Visualize thermohaline circulation - the global conveyor belt driven by temperature and salinity differences that shapes Earth's climate.

 05

Volcanic Eruption

Particle-based simulation of pyroclastic flows, lava fountains, and ash clouds. Adjust eruption intensity and watch the volcanic spectacle.

 06

Glacier Dynamics

Ice sheet flow, accumulation zones, ablation, and calving events. See how glaciers shape valleys and respond to climate change.

 07

Earthquake Waves

P-waves, S-waves, and surface waves radiate from the epicenter. Interactive visualization of seismic wave propagation and ground motion.

 08

Water Cycle

Follow water through evaporation, condensation, precipitation, and runoff. See the continuous movement of Earth's most vital resource.

 09

Carbon Cycle

Interactive Sankey diagram showing carbon flows between atmosphere, oceans, biosphere, and geosphere. Explore the global carbon budget.

 10

Milankovitch Cycles

Earth's orbital variations (eccentricity, obliquity, precession) that drive ice ages over thousands of years. See climate cycles emerge.

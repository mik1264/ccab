The Science of Consciousness Reaches a Crossroads

In April 2025, the Cogitate Consortium published a landmark adversarial collaboration in Nature, testing two leading theories of consciousness: Integrated Information Theory (IIT) and Global Neuronal Workspace Theory (GNWT). The result was a "draw" — both theories faced critical empirical challenges.

These interactive demonstrations explore the science, the theories, the experiments, and the profound implications for understanding awareness — in humans, animals, and artificial intelligence.

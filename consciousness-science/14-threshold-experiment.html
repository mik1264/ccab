Consciousness Threshold Experiment

This experiment simulates how researchers measure the threshold at which stimuli become conscious.

  • A shape will flash very briefly on screen
  • Report whether you saw the shape or not
  • The duration will adjust based on your responses (staircase method)
  • We'll find your consciousness threshold

The experiment takes about 2 minutes (30 trials).

What this take shows. Two clusters of agents with structurally asymmetric distortion. For in-group targets, Funder-feel grievances toward members of their own cluster (suppression: "I'm not really angry at her"). For out-group targets, F>G — agents over-feel grievances across the bridge (projection, attribution error). The rumor mechanic: high projection in one A-member contagiously raises projection in adjacent A-members.

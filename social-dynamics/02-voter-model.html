How It Works

At each time step, a random agent copies the opinion of a random neighbor. This simple rule leads to:

• Random drift: Opinions fluctuate randomly
• Eventual consensus: One opinion dominates (in finite systems)
• Cluster formation: Spatial regions of agreement emerge

Social Dynamics

Simulating human behavior, collective action, and social emergence

About Social Dynamics Simulations

Explore the emergent patterns of human social behavior through agent-based models. From opinion formation and voting systems to cultural evolution and trust networks, these simulations reveal how individual decisions aggregate into collective outcomes. Watch segregation emerge from simple preferences, see languages evolve through interaction, and understand how rumors spread through populations.

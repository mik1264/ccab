Spiral of Silence

Elisabeth Noelle-Neumann's theory: People with minority opinions become silent when they perceive they're outnumbered, amplifying majority dominance even when opinions are initially balanced.

How Silence Spirals

Each person has a private opinion (A or B). They become silent if their local environment shows strong support for the opposing view.

• Bright: Vocally expressing opinion
• Dark: Silent (self-censoring)
• Red vs Blue: Opinion A vs B

High pressure → Minority quickly silenced!

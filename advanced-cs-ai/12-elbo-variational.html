ELBO Decomposition

ELBO = E_q[log p(x,z)] - E_q[log q(z)]
= E_q[log p(x|z)] - KL(q(z) || p(z))

Key Insight

The evidence log p(x) is fixed. Maximizing ELBO is equivalent to minimizing KL(q || p), making q closer to the true posterior.

Oregonator Model

The Oregonator is a simplified chemical kinetics model of the BZ reaction with three variables: HBrO₂ (X), Br⁻ (Y), and Ce⁴⁺ (Z).

dX/dt = s(Y - XY + X - qX²)
dY/dt = (-Y - XY + fZ) / s
dZ/dt = w(X - Z)

Shows oscillatory behavior and spatial pattern formation.

Luminol Chemiluminescence

Reaction: Luminol + H2O2 + Fe(hemoglobin)

Emission: 425nm (blue)

Stains Found: 0 / ?

Glow Duration: ~30 seconds

Move your cursor to spray luminol across the darkened room. Iron in hemoglobin catalyzes the oxidation, producing blue-green chemiluminescence. The glow fades after ~30 seconds.

# 100 New Simulation & Visualization Ideas

A curated list of 100 distinct simulation and visualization ideas that expand upon the existing CCAB catalog. These ideas focus on underserved domains such as advanced engineering, modern AI architectures, linguistics, quantum computing algorithms, and complex biological systems.

---

## I. Advanced Computer Science & AI (10)
1.  **Transformer Attention Head Visualizer**: Interactive visualization of self-attention matrices in a mini-GPT model, showing how tokens attend to each other during generation.
2.  **Raft Consensus Simulator**: A visual node-based simulation of the Raft distributed consensus algorithm, showing leader election and log replication.
3.  **Garbage Collection Algorithms**: Visual comparison of Mark-and-Sweep, Reference Counting, and Generational GC memory management in real-time.
4.  **Database B-Tree Splitting**: Interactive step-by-step visualization of B-Tree and B+Tree insertions, causing node splits and rebalancing.
5.  **Git Merkle Tree Explorer**: Visualizing the DAG (Directed Acyclic Graph) of git commits, showing how hashes propagate and how branches diverge/merge.
6.  **Bloom Filter Probabilities**: Interactive demonstration of hash collisions and false positive rates in Bloom filters as capacity fills.
7.  **Kalman Filter Navigator**: A 2D simulation showing a noisy sensor tracking an object, visualizing the predict-update cycle and covariance ellipses.
8.  **Regular Expression Engine**: Visual state machine (NFA/DFA) construction from regex patterns, animating the matching process against text.
9.  **Instruction Pipelining**: Visualization of a CPU 5-stage pipeline (Fetch, Decode, Execute, Mem, WB) showing bubbles/stalls and hazard detection.
10. **Public Key Cryptography (RSA)**: Visual step-by-step arithmetic of RSA key generation, encryption, and decryption using small prime numbers.

## II. Physics: Quantum & Relativity (10)
11. **Special Relativity Time Dilation**: A simulation where moving clocks run slower relative to a stationary observer, visualized with light clocks.
12. **Lorentz Contraction Racer**: Visualizing length contraction of objects moving near $c$, including the visual distortion (Terrell rotation).
13. **Michelson-Morley Interferometer**: Interactive setup of the famous experiment, showing interference fringe shifts (or lack thereof).
14. **Quantum Key Distribution (BB84)**: A gamified simulation of Alice and Bob exchanging qubits, detecting Eve's eavesdropping via error rates.
15. **Bloch Sphere Playground**: Interactive 3D visualization of single-qubit gates (Hadamard, Pauli-X/Y/Z) rotating the state vector.
16. **Grover's Search Algorithm**: Visualizing the amplitude amplification process in a quantum search database.
17. **Stern-Gerlach Experiment**: Particle spin quantization visualization, showing splitting of silver atoms in a magnetic field.
18. **Black Body Radiation**: Interactive Planck curve slider changing temperature, visualizing color shift and peak wavelength (Wien's Law).
19. **Photoelectric Effect**: Simulation of photons hitting a metal plate, showing the frequency threshold for electron emission.
20. **Feynman Diagrams**: Drag-and-drop constructor for simple particle interactions, validating conservation laws (charge, lepton number).

## III. Engineering & Robotics (10)
21. **Inverse Kinematics (IK) Solver**: Interactive 3-joint robotic arm that follows the mouse cursor, solving joint angles in real-time.
22. **PID Controller Tuning**: A line-following robot simulation where users tune P, I, and D gains to minimize oscillation and error.
23. **Truss Bridge Stress Analysis**: Build-your-own bridge tool that visualizes compression (red) and tension (blue) members under load.
24. **Four-Bar Linkage Designer**: Interactive synthesis of mechanical linkages to trace specific coupler curves.
25. **Gear Ratio Simulator**: Visualizing torque vs. speed trade-offs in compound gear trains and planetary gears.
26. **Op-Amp Circuits**: Interactive analog circuit simulator for Inverting, Non-inverting, and Integrator operational amplifier configurations.
27. **Logic Gate Sandbox**: Drag-and-drop digital logic circuit builder (AND, OR, NOT, XOR) with live signal propagation.
28. **Stepping Motor Physics**: Visualizing the electromagnetic switching sequence of a stepper motor rotor.
29. **Hydraulic Press Pascal's Principle**: Interactive demo of force multiplication in hydraulic systems with different piston areas.
30. **Aerofoil Wind Tunnel**: 2D fluid flow over different wing shapes, visualizing lift, drag, and stall angles.

## IV. Biology & Medicine (10)
31. **CRISPR-Cas9 Editing**: Visual simulation of the Cas9 protein scanning DNA, matching the guide RNA, and cutting the strand.
32. **Neuron Action Potential**: Interactive voltage-gated ion channel simulation showing depolarization, repolarization, and the refractory period.
33. **Antibody-Antigen Binding**: Molecular dynamics simulation of immune system specificity and affinity maturation.
34. **Circadian Rhythm Molecular Clock**: Simulation of the PER/CRY feedback loop regulating biological time.
35. **Horizontal Gene Transfer**: Bacterial simulation showing plasmid exchange (conjugation) conferring antibiotic resistance.
36. **Blood Type Compatibility**: Visual transfusion game logic based on Antigen/Antibody interactions (ABO and Rh systems).
37. **Muscle Contraction (Sliding Filament)**: Animation of Actin and Myosin cross-bridge cycling driven by ATP.
38. **Synaptic Transmission**: Visualizing neurotransmitter release, diffusion across the cleft, and receptor binding.
39. **Kidney Nephron Function**: Filtration, reabsorption, and secretion visualization along the Loop of Henle.
40. **Mendelian Cross Simulator**: Interactive Punnett square generator for dihybrid crosses with dominant/recessive traits.

## V. Chemistry & Materials Science (10)
41. **Lennard-Jones Potential**: Molecular dynamics simulation of atoms interacting via van der Waals forces, showing states of matter.
42. **Chirality Explorer**: 3D interactive comparison of enantiomers (mirror-image molecules) and their non-superimposable nature.
43. **Electrolysis Simulation**: Visualizing ion migration to anode/cathode and redox reactions in an electrolytic cell.
44. **Chromatography Column**: Simulation of separation based on molecule polarity and stationary phase interaction.
45. **Phase Diagram Explorer**: Interactive Water/CO2 phase diagrams, visualizing the triple point and critical point.
46. **Crystal Lattice Builder**: 3D visualization of Bravais lattices (FCC, BCC, HCP) and unit cell packing efficiency.
47. **Polymerization**: Simulation of chain-growth vs. step-growth polymerization kinetics.
48. **Buffer Solution pH**: Visualizing the Henderson-Hasselbalch equation and resistance to pH change upon acid/base addition.
49. **Spectroscopy Absorption**: Visualizing electron energy level transitions corresponding to absorption spectra lines.
50. **Semiconductor Doping**: Visualizing N-type vs. P-type doping and the formation of a PN junction depletion region.

## VI. Earth & Space Science (10)
51. **Seismic Wave Propagation**: Simulation of P-waves (longitudinal) and S-waves (transverse) traveling through Earth's layers.
52. **Plate Tectonics Boundaries**: Visualizing Convergent, Divergent, and Transform faults and their resulting geological features.
53. **Coriolis Effect**: Interactive particle throw on a rotating Earth, visualizing deflection in Northern vs. Southern hemispheres.
54. **Milankovitch Cycles**: Visualizing Earth's orbital eccentricity, axial tilt, and precession cycles affecting climate over millennia.
55. **Exoplanet Transit Method**: Simulating light curve dips as planets of different sizes cross in front of stars.
56. **Radial Velocity Method**: Visualizing the "wobble" of a star (Doppler shift) caused by orbiting planets.
57. **Atmospheric Lapse Rate**: Interactive balloon ascent showing temperature changes in Troposphere, Stratosphere, etc.
58. **Ocean Thermohaline Circulation**: Simulation of the global conveyor belt driven by temperature and salinity density differences.
59. **Lunar Phases & Libration**: Interactive 3D moon orbit showing why we see phases and the "wobble" (libration).
60. **Retrograde Motion**: Top-down vs. Earth-view visualization explaining the apparent backward motion of Mars.

## VII. Mathematics & Geometry (10)
61. **Non-Euclidean Hyperbolic Geometry**: Interactive Poincaré disk model where lines are arcs and angles are preserved.
62. **Mobius Strip Painter**: 3D interactive strip allowing painting to prove it has only one side and one edge.
63. **Klein Bottle Topology**: 3D visualization of the non-orientable surface with no inside or outside.
64. **Riemann Sums**: Interactive integration visualizer comparing Left, Right, Midpoint, and Trapezoidal approximations.
65. **Eigenvectors & Eigenvalues**: Linear transformation visualizer showing vectors that span the same line after transformation.
66. **Bayes' Theorem Geometric**: Area-based visualization of conditional probabilities (P(A|B) vs P(B|A)).
67. **Collatz Conjecture Tree**: Visualizing the "3n+1" sequences as a directed graph, searching for loops.
68. **Ulam Spiral Explorer**: Prime number spiral visualization highlighting diagonal patterns.
69. **Tessellation Editor**: Interactive playground for the 17 wallpaper groups (plane symmetry groups).
70. **Quaternions Rotation**: Visualizing 3D rotation using 4D quaternions (avoiding Gimbal lock).

## VIII. Social Science & Humanities (10)
71. **Gerrymandering Simulator**: Interactive map redistricting game to favor one party over another using "packing" and "cracking".
72. **Supply Chain Bullwhip Effect**: Multi-stage beer game simulation showing how demand fluctuations amplify upstream.
73. **Voting Systems Comparison**: Simulating First-Past-The-Post, Ranked Choice, and Approval voting on the same population distribution.
74. **Tragedy of the Commons**: ABM of herdsmen on a shared pasture, exploring strategies to prevent resource depletion.
75. **Language Evolution Drift**: Simulation of vowel shifts or word meaning drift in isolated populations over generations.
76. **Six Degrees of Kevin Bacon**: Small-world network simulation showing shortest path connections in social graphs.
77. **Market Maker Order Book**: Visualizing a Limit Order Book (LOB) with bid/ask spread and depth chart dynamics.
78. **Traffic Shockwaves**: Phantom traffic jam simulation on a ring road caused by a single braking event.
79. **Diffusion of Innovations**: Everett Rogers' adoption curve (Innovators to Laggards) simulation in a social network.
80. **Braess's Paradox**: Traffic simulation showing how adding a road can actually slow down overall traffic flow.

## IX. Acoustics & Signal Processing (10)
81. **Doppler Effect Audio**: Moving sound source simulation with real-time frequency shifting and wavefront visualization.
82. **Room Acoustics Ray Tracing**: Visualizing sound reflection, absorption, and reverberation in rooms of different shapes.
83. **Nyquist-Shannon Sampling**: Interactive demo showing aliasing effects when sampling rate falls below 2x frequency.
84. **AM/FM Modulation**: Visual comparison of Amplitude and Frequency modulation of a carrier wave by a signal.
85. **Guitar String Harmonics**: Visualizing standing wave nodes and antinodes for fundamental frequency and overtones.
86. **Head-Related Transfer Function (HRTF)**: Visualizing how ear shape filters sound direction (spatial audio).
87. **Fourier Series Drawing**: Drawing arbitrary 2D paths by summing rotating phasors (interactive implementation).
88. **Spectrogram Waterfall**: Real-time microphone input visualization with time-frequency-intensity heat map.
89. **Active Noise Control**: Simulating destructive interference to cancel out a noise wave.
90. **Sonoluminescence**: Visualization of sound waves creating collapsing bubbles that emit light.

## X. Art & Experimental (10)
91. **Impossible Object Rotator**: 3D rendering tricks to visualize Penrose triangles and Escher staircases.
92. **Slit-Scan Photography**: Time-displacement video effect ("scanner camera") applied to real-time webcam feed.
93. **Dithering Algorithms**: Interactive comparison of Floyd-Steinberg, Atkinson, and Ordered dithering on images.
94. **Anamorphic Illusion Generator**: Distorting an image so it looks correct only from a specific steep angle (3D chalk art style).
95. **Moiré Pattern Generator**: Interactive overlapping grids/circles creating interference patterns.
96. **Pixel Sorting**: Glitch art generator sorting pixels by brightness/hue within threshold masks.
97. **Data Moshing Simulator**: Visualizing video compression artifacts (I-frame removal) for artistic effect.
98. **Reaction-Diffusion Texture Synthesizer**: Using Gray-Scott to generate realistic animal coat textures (expanding current demo).
99. **Cellular Automata Music**: Generating MIDI sequences from 1D cellular automata rule evolution.
100. **ASCII Art Video Converter**: Real-time density mapping of camera input to character sets.

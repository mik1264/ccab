Branchial Entropy & Fan-Out

In Wolfram's multiway system, each step a string can be rewritten in many ways, fanning out a graph of distinct successor states. The branchial entropy at step t is log₂(number of distinct states at t). Rules with low fan-out feel near-classical; rules with explosive fan-out generate the rich superposition space Wolfram identifies with quantum behavior.

Observer Splitting

When an observer reaches a multiway branch and cannot resolve which thread to commit to, they split into multiple observer-instances — one per branch — each subsequently experiencing its own history. This is the Everett “many-worlds” intuition, but rendered inside Wolfram’s branchial space.

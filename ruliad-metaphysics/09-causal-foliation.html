Causal Foliation

In Wolfram's ruliad, the same fixed causal graph of events can be sliced into "moments of now" in many different valid ways — each foliation is one observer's choice of reference frame, and tilting the slice changes what "happens together." Drag the terracotta line, sweep it downward, and compare two observers' snapshots side by side.

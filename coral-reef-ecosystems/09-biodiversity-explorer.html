Reef Biodiversity

Coral reefs cover less than 0.1% of the ocean floor but support 25% of all marine species. This cross-section shows the major functional groups: producers (coral, algae), herbivores (parrotfish, urchins, tangs), planktivores (damselfish), predators (grouper, moray, sharks), and cleaners (wrasses).

Reef Trophic Cascade

Parrotfish graze algae, keeping reefs healthy. Groupers control parrotfish numbers. Overfishing groupers causes parrotfish population explosion -- but overfishing parrotfish lets algae smother corals. Crown-of-thorns starfish eat coral directly.

Ecosystem Connectivity

Mangroves, seagrass beds, and coral reefs form an interconnected tropical seascape. Juvenile reef fish use mangroves as nurseries. Seagrass stabilizes sediment and filters nutrients. Reefs with adjacent mangroves have 2x the fish biomass and 50% less coral disease. Removing any habitat cascades through the system.

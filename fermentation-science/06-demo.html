Kimchi Fermentation

Kimchi Microbial Succession

Kimchi fermentation follows a predictable microbial succession. Leuconostoc mesenteroides dominates early (heterofermentative, CO2-producing). As pH drops, Lactobacillus plantarum takes over (homofermentative, acid-tolerant). Salt concentration, temperature, and spices shape the final microbial community and flavor.

MLF Control

Malolactic Fermentation (MLF)

HOOC-CH2-CHOH-COOH → CH3-CHOH-COOH + CO2

Oenococcus oeni converts sharp dicarboxylic malic acid into softer monocarboxylic lactic acid. This secondary fermentation softens wine acidity and adds buttery complexity (diacetyl). MLF is inhibited by low pH, high SO2, and cold temperatures.

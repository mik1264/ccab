Understanding the conductor of elliptic curves and its prime factorization

About Conductors
The conductor N of an elliptic curve encodes information about primes where the curve has "bad reduction."

For primes p not dividing N, the curve has good reduction mod p, and we can compute a_p values.

Curves are often grouped by conductor when studying murmurations.

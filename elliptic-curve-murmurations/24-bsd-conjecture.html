The Conjecture

BSD predicts that the algebraic rank of E(Q) equals the analytic rank (order of vanishing of L(E,s) at s=1). This connects arithmetic and analysis in a profound way.

Murmuration Connection

The distinct oscillation patterns for rank 0 vs rank 1 curves provide evidence for BSD—the curves "know" their rank through their collective aₚ behavior.

Millennium Prize

BSD is one of seven Millennium Prize Problems. A complete proof would earn $1,000,000 and revolutionize our understanding of elliptic curves.

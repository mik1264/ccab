Rank 0 vs Rank 1 Curves

The fundamental distinction that creates murmuration patterns

Rank 0 Finitely many rational points
Rank 1 Infinitely many rational points

What is Rank?

The rank of an elliptic curve is the number of independent generators of its group of rational points. Rank 0 means only finitely many (torsion) points; rank 1+ means infinitely many.

Why Does Rank Matter?

When researchers averaged a_p values separately for rank 0 and rank 1 curves, they discovered distinct oscillating patterns—the murmurations. This separation was key to the discovery!

The Birch-SD Conjecture

This famous Millennium Prize problem predicts that rank equals the order of vanishing of the L-function at s=1. Murmurations may provide new insights.

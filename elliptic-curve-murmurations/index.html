About Murmurations

Named after the mesmerizing flight patterns of starlings, "murmurations" in number theory describe unexpected wave-like patterns discovered when plotting normalized Frobenius traces (a‚Çö/‚àöp) of elliptic curves. The discovery was made by Alexey Pozdnyakov while working with Yang-Hui He's team using machine learning to study elliptic curve data from the LMFDB. Nina Zubrilina later proved an explicit formula involving Airy functions. This gallery explores these patterns through 40 interactive visualizations.

Kawasaki's Theorem

At any interior vertex of a flat-foldable crease pattern, the alternating sum of angles equals zero. Equivalently: odd angles sum to 180° and even angles sum to 180°.

Click to place crease lines radiating from center. Drag endpoints to adjust angles. The theorem checks flat-foldability in real-time.

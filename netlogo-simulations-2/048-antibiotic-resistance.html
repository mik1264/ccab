üíä Antibiotic Resistance

Antibiotic resistance evolves when bacteria survive treatment and pass on resistance genes.

Selection Pressure

  • Antibiotics kill sensitive bacteria
  • Resistant mutants survive and multiply
  • Resistance spreads through population

Fitness Trade-offs

  • Resistance often has a fitness cost
  • Without antibiotics, sensitive outcompete resistant
  • With antibiotics, resistant have advantage

Implications

Overuse of antibiotics accelerates resistance. Antibiotic stewardship programs aim to reduce unnecessary use.

Edge effects are changes in species composition and ecological conditions at habitat boundaries.

Edge vs Core Habitat

  • Core: Interior habitat, stable microclimate
  • Edge: Boundary zone with altered conditions
  • Edge penetration depth varies by effect

Microclimate Changes

  • Higher light, temperature, wind at edges
  • Lower humidity, different soil moisture
  • Increased disturbance (wind throw, fire)

Species Responses

  • Interior specialists: Decline with fragmentation
  • Edge specialists: Thrive in fragmented landscapes
  • Predators, parasites often increase at edges

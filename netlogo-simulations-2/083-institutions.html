Institutions are the rules of the game in society - both formal laws and informal norms that constrain behavior.

Emergence Process

  • Informal Norms: Repeated interactions create expectations
  • Formalization: Norms codified into explicit rules
  • Enforcement: Third-party punishment mechanisms
  • Legitimacy: Rules internalized and self-enforcing

Key Dynamics

  • Path dependence - early choices constrain later options
  • Enforcement costs vs. benefits of cooperation
  • Critical mass for norm stability
  • Institutional change and resistance

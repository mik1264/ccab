Gossip Protocols

Gossip protocols (epidemic protocols) spread information through random peer-to-peer communication.

Key properties:

  • Fanout: Number of peers each node contacts per round
  • Exponential spread: O(log N) rounds for full propagation
  • Fault tolerance: Works despite random node failures
  • Probabilistic guarantees: High probability of delivery

Used in distributed databases, blockchain networks, and cluster membership.

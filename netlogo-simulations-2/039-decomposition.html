Decomposition is the breakdown of organic matter by decomposers, recycling nutrients back into ecosystems.

Decomposer Community

  • Bacteria: Fast-growing, prefer high-quality litter
  • Fungi: Can break down lignin and cellulose
  • Invertebrates: Shredders fragment litter, increasing surface area

Controlling Factors

  • Temperature: Higher = faster (to a point)
  • Moisture: Optimal around 50-70%
  • C:N Ratio: Lower = faster decomposition

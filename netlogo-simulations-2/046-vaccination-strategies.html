Vaccination strategies determine who gets vaccinated when supply is limited.

Strategy Types

  • Mass: Vaccinate everyone systematically
  • Ring: Vaccinate contacts of cases
  • Targeted: Prioritize high-risk groups
  • Random: No prioritization

Trade-offs

  • Ring vaccination works well for slow diseases
  • Targeted reaches vulnerable but may miss spreaders
  • Mass is equitable but may be slow

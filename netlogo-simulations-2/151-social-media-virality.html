Social Media Virality

Viral content spreads exponentially through social networks based on engagement dynamics.

Key factors:

  • R0 (reproduction rate): Average reshares per post
  • Influencers: High-follower accounts amplify reach
  • Network structure: Clustering affects spread patterns
  • Content decay: Interest decreases over time

Larger nodes are influencers with more followers and higher share boost.

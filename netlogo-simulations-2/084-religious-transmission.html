Cultural transmission of religious beliefs involves multiple mechanisms that affect adoption and persistence.

Transmission Modes

  • Vertical: Parent to child (most stable)
  • Horizontal: Peer to peer
  • Oblique: Non-parent adults to children

Costly Signaling Theory

Expensive rituals and sacrifices signal genuine commitment, building trust within religious communities.

CREDs

Credibility-Enhancing Displays (CREDs) are actions that demonstrate sincere belief, making transmission more effective.

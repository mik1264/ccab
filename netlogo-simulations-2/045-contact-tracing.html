Contact tracing identifies and quarantines people exposed to confirmed cases, breaking transmission chains.

Process

  • Case confirmed ‚Üí contacts identified
  • Contacts notified and quarantined
  • Prevents onward transmission

Key Factors

  • Speed: Faster tracing = more effective
  • Coverage: % of contacts successfully traced
  • Compliance: Quarantine adherence
  • Pre-symptomatic: Transmission before detection

Digital vs Manual

Apps can speed notification but face adoption/privacy challenges. Manual tracing is slower but can probe deeper.

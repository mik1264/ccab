Fake News Spreading

Misinformation spreads through social networks influenced by confirmation bias and trust dynamics.

Key dynamics:

  • Gullibility: How easily people believe unverified claims
  • Echo chambers: Reinforcement within like-minded groups
  • Fact-checking: Rate at which claims are verified
  • Continued influence: Debunking doesn't fully undo belief

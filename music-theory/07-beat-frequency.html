What are Beat Frequencies?

When two sounds with slightly different frequencies are played together, they interfere and create a pulsing effect called "beats." The beat frequency equals the difference between the two frequencies. This is how musicians tune instruments - when the beats slow down and disappear, the notes are in tune!

Beat Frequency = |f₁ - f₂| = |440 - 444| = 4 Hz

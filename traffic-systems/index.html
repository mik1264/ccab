About Traffic Systems Simulations

Explore the fascinating dynamics of traffic flow through interactive simulations. From highway congestion and intersection timing to public transit networks and parking optimization, these visualizations reveal the emergent patterns that arise from simple vehicle interactions. Discover how phantom traffic jams form, why roundabouts are efficient, and how traffic signals can be optimized.

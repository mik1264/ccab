2D Ice Sheet Model

A continental ice sheet spreads under its own weight. The Shallow Ice Approximation computes ice flux from surface slope and thickness. Snow accumulates at high latitudes and melts at the margins. The dome-shaped profile is characteristic of real ice sheets like Greenland and Antarctica.

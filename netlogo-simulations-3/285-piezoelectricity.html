Piezoelectricity

Piezoelectric effect: mechanical stress → electric polarization (direct), or electric field → strain (converse). Dipoles in non-centrosymmetric crystals respond to applied forces/fields.

Polarization P: 0 μC/cm²
Electric Field E: 0 kV/cm
Strain ε: 0 × 10⁻⁶
Output: 0
Direct Effect: σ → P → V

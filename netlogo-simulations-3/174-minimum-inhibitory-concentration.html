MIC Determination

The Minimum Inhibitory Concentration is the lowest antibiotic concentration that prevents visible bacterial growth. Below MIC, bacteria grow; at/above MIC, growth is inhibited. Kill curves show how bacterial population changes over time at different concentrations.

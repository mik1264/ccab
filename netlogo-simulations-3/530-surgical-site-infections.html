Health emerges from complex interactions between biology, behavior, and healthcare systems. This simulation models disease dynamics and medical interventions.

About This Simulation

Implement a surgical infection prevention model.

Key Concepts

  • Epidemiology: How diseases spread through populations, described by compartmental models (SIR, SEIR).
  • Treatment Optimization: Finding the best intervention strategies considering efficacy, side effects, and resources.
  • Hospital Dynamics: Patient flow, resource allocation, and capacity planning in healthcare facilities.
  • Behavior Change: How health behaviors spread and can be influenced through interventions.

Why It Matters

Health modeling improves pandemic response, treatment protocols, and healthcare resource allocation.

How to Explore

  • Adjust the sliders to modify simulation parameters and observe how the system responds
  • Look for emergent patterns that arise from agent interactions
  • Try extreme parameter values to find phase transitions and tipping points
  • Compare the simulation behavior to real-world phenomena

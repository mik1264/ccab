Microbial Fuel Cell

Electroactive bacteria (like Geobacter, Shewanella) transfer electrons from organic matter oxidation to electrodes. Electrons flow through an external circuit, generating electricity. Biofilm formation on the anode enhances electron transfer via conductive pili (nanowires).

Bacterial Motility

Swimming: Flagella rotate to propel through liquid. Swarming: Hyperflagellated cells move in coordinated rafts on surfaces. Twitching: Type IV pili extend, attach, and retract. Gliding: Focal adhesion complexes drive smooth surface movement without external appendages.

Bioremediation

Bioremediation uses microorganisms to degrade environmental pollutants. Generalist degraders break down many compounds slowly, while specialists efficiently target specific pollutants. Community interactions create metabolic consortia that achieve complete detoxification through sequential degradation pathways.

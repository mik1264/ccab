Menu Costs

Menu costs are expenses of changing prices (printing new menus, signage). Firms only adjust prices when the gain exceeds these costs, creating nominal rigidity. This leads to prices that respond slowly to demand changes, contributing to real effects of monetary policy.

Key Concepts

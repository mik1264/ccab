Dysbiosis

Dysbiosis is an imbalance in gut microbiome composition. Disturbances (antibiotics, diet change, stress) kill beneficial bacteria, allowing pathobionts (normally harmless residents that become harmful) and pathogens to bloom. Recovery depends on colonization resistance from remaining beneficial species.

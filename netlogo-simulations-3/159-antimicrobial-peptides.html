Antimicrobial Peptides

AMPs are host defense molecules produced by epithelial cells. They disrupt bacterial membranes through electrostatic interactions. Bacteria can evolve resistance through membrane modifications. AMPs provide rapid, broad-spectrum defense at mucosal surfaces.

Bacteriophage Life Cycles

Lytic cycle: Phage infects, replicates, and lyses the host, releasing new phages (burst).
Lysogenic cycle: Phage DNA integrates into bacterial chromosome as a prophage. The lysogen is immune to further infection. Stress can trigger induction, switching to lytic cycle.

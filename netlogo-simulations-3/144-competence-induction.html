Natural Competence

Some bacteria can enter a "competent" state where they can take up extracellular DNA. This is triggered by stress conditions (starvation, antibiotics). Taken-up DNA can be integrated into the chromosome through homologous recombination, enabling horizontal gene transfer. Species like Streptococcus pneumoniae and Bacillus subtilis are naturally competent.

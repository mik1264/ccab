Phage Therapy

Bacteriophages are viruses that specifically infect bacteria. In therapy, phages are applied to kill pathogenic bacteria. Key dynamics: phage-bacteria encounter, infection, replication, and lysis releasing new phages. Self-amplifying nature makes phages effective against resistant bacteria.

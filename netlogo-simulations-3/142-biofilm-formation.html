Biofilm Development

1. Attachment: Planktonic cells reversibly adhere to surface.
2. Colonization: Irreversible attachment and microcolony formation.
3. Maturation: EPS matrix production, 3D structure development.
4. Dispersal: Cells detach to colonize new surfaces.

Lichen Symbiosis

Lichens are symbioses between fungi (mycobiont) and algae or cyanobacteria (photobiont). The photobiont provides sugars via photosynthesis; the fungus provides structure, protection, and mineral acquisition. This partnership can colonize extreme environments.

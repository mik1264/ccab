Climate Feedback Mechanisms

Positive feedbacks amplify warming (e.g., water vapor, ice-albedo), while negative feedbacks dampen it (e.g., Planck radiation). The net feedback determines climate sensitivity - how much warming results from doubled CO₂. Cloud feedback remains the largest source of uncertainty in climate projections.

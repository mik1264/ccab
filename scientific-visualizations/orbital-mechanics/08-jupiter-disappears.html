Jupiter Disappears

What happens to Jupiter's 95 moons if the planet suddenly vanishes? Without Jupiter's gravity, each moon continues tangentially at its orbital velocity.

Inner moons fall sunward. Outer retrograde moons may escape the solar system entirely.

Contact tracing identifies and quarantines individuals who had contact with confirmed cases, breaking transmission chains.

Key Factors

  • Efficiency: Proportion of contacts successfully traced
  • Speed: Delay between detection and quarantine
  • Coverage: Fraction of population participating

Digital vs Traditional

  • Traditional: Interviews, slower but thorough
  • Digital apps: Faster but requires adoption
  • Hybrid approaches most effective

Effectiveness

Can reduce R below 1 if combined with testing. Most effective early in outbreak when case counts are low.

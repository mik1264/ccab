A food web represents feeding relationships in an ecosystem. Network properties affect stability and resilience.

Network Properties

  • Connectance: Fraction of possible links that exist
  • Trophic level: Position in food chain (1=producer, 2+=consumers)
  • Nestedness: Generalists interact with species specialists do

Cascade Dynamics

Removing species can trigger extinction cascades. Highly connected species (keystones) cause larger cascades when removed.

Niche Model

Species are ordered by "niche value" and consume prey within a range below their niche position, producing realistic web structure.

The ideal gas law PV = nRT relates pressure, volume, temperature, and amount of gas.

Kinetic Theory

  • Gas = many particles in random motion
  • Collisions are elastic
  • Particle size negligible vs container
  • No intermolecular forces

Key Relationships

  • Pressure: Force from wall collisions
  • Temperature: Average kinetic energy
  • KE = (3/2)kT per molecule

Gas Laws

  • Boyle: PV = constant (at fixed T)
  • Charles: V/T = constant (at fixed P)
  • Gay-Lussac: P/T = constant (at fixed V)

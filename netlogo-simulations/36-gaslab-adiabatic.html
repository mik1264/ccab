Adiabatic Piston

No heat exchange with surroundings. Rapid compression heats the gas (TV^(γ-1) = const). Watch temperature change with volume!

Adiabatic: Q=0, so work done on gas increases internal energy (temperature).

Compare: Fast compression is adiabatic. Slow compression allows heat to escape (approaches isothermal).

GasLab Adiabatic Piston

Adiabatic compression/expansion: no heat exchange with surroundings. When gas is compressed rapidly, work done increases internal energy, raising temperature. Expansion cools the gas. Demonstrates PV^γ = constant.

TV^(γ-1) = constant
γ = Cp/Cv ≈ 1.4 (diatomic)

Pressure: 1.0 atm
Volume: 100%
PV^γ: 1.00

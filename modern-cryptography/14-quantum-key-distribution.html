How BB84 Works

1. Quantum Transmission: Alice sends photons polarized according to random bits and bases.

2. Random Measurement: Bob measures with randomly chosen bases (correct basis = correct bit).

3. Basis Reconciliation: Alice and Bob publicly compare bases (not bits!) and keep only matching ones.

4. Eavesdropper Detection: Any interception disturbs quantum states, introducing detectable errors.

The security is guaranteed by quantum mechanics - measurement disturbs the state!

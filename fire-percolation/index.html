About Fire Percolation

Developed by Uri Wilensky (1997), the Forest Fire model demonstrates how simple cellular dynamics produce complex phase transition phenomena. At the percolation threshold (p_c ‚âà 0.59 for Von Neumann lattice), systems exhibit critical behavior: power-law distributions, fractal geometry, and diverging correlation lengths. This gallery makes these abstract concepts tangible through interactive visualization.

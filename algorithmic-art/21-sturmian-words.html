About Sturmian Words

A Sturmian word is generated by the cutting sequence of a line y = αx + β with irrational slope α. As x increases, we write '0' when crossing a horizontal grid line and '1' when crossing a vertical grid line.

Balance property: For any two factors of the same length, the number of 1s differs by at most 1.

Complexity: Exactly n+1 distinct factors of length n (minimal for aperiodic sequences).

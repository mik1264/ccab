Gamelan Scales: Pelog & Slendro

Slendro: 5 roughly-equal steps per octave (~240 cents each). No Western equivalent.

Pelog: 7 unequal steps with large and small intervals. Haunting, shimmering quality.

Every gamelan ensemble is tuned slightly differently -- there's no single standard. Click the metallophones to play.

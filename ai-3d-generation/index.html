AI 3D Generation

8 interactive demonstrations

Tripo, Hunyuan3D & the AI 3D Revolution

Modern AI tools like Tripo and Tencent's Hunyuan3D turn text prompts or single images into production-ready 3D meshes in seconds. These demos visualise the core ideas behind that pipeline — diffusion in latent space, multi-view synthesis, voxel-to-mesh extraction, PBR texturing, and topology generation — using procedural Three.js scenes that you can poke, prompt, and rotate.

Network Dynamics

Explore the fascinating world of complex networks through 10 interactive simulations. From cascade failures to community detection, see how networks evolve, spread information, and respond to disruption using force-directed graph visualizations.

01

Cascade Failures

Watch how single node failures trigger cascading failures throughout interconnected networks, similar to power grid blackouts.

 02

Information Diffusion

See how memes, news, and ideas spread through social networks using epidemic-style diffusion models.

 03

Network Resilience

Compare how different network topologies (random, scale-free, small-world) handle random vs targeted attacks.

 04

Preferential Attachment

Watch the Barabási-Albert model in action as new nodes preferentially connect to popular hubs—the "rich get richer" phenomenon.

 05

Community Detection

Discover hidden communities in networks using the Louvain algorithm, revealing modular structures and clusters.

 06

Network Centrality

Compare different centrality measures—degree, betweenness, and PageRank—to identify the most important nodes.

 07

Small World Networks

Explore the "six degrees of separation" through pathfinding in small-world networks with high clustering and short paths.

 08

Network Motifs

Identify and highlight recurring structural patterns like triangles, feed-forward loops, and other fundamental building blocks.

 09

Link Prediction

Predict which nodes are likely to form connections based on common neighbors, structural similarity, and network topology.

 10

Temporal Networks

Watch networks evolve over time as nodes and edges appear, disappear, and change, revealing dynamic patterns and phases.

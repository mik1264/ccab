Aries

Name: The Ram

Abbreviation: Ari

Zodiac: Yes (March 21 - April 19)

Best Seen: December

Mythology: The golden ram that rescued Phrixus and Helle. Its golden fleece became the quest of Jason and the Argonauts.

Notable Stars:
• Hamal (α Ari) - "The head of the ram"
• Sheratan (β Ari) - "The two signs"
• Mesarthim (γ Ari) - First double star discovered by telescope
• Ancient location of vernal equinox

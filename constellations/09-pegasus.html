Pegasus

Name: The Winged Horse

Abbreviation: Peg

Best Seen: October

Mythology: Divine winged horse born from Medusa's blood when Perseus killed her. Helped Bellerophon defeat the Chimera.

Notable Features:
• Great Square of Pegasus - Famous asterism
• 51 Pegasi - First sun-like star found with exoplanet (1995)
• Enif (ε Peg) - Orange supergiant

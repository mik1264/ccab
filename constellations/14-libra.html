Libra

Name: The Scales

Abbreviation: Lib

Zodiac: Yes (September 23 - October 22)

Best Seen: June

Mythology: The scales of justice held by Themis or Astraea. Only zodiac constellation representing an inanimate object.

Notable Stars:
• Zubeneschamali (β Lib) - Only green star visible to naked eye
• Zubenelgenubi (α Lib) - "Southern Claw"
• Gliese 581 - System with multiple exoplanets

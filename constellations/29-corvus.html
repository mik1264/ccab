Corvus

Name: The Crow

Abbreviation: Crv

Hemisphere: Southern

Best Seen: May

Mythology: Apollo's crow, sent to watch over his lover. Forms a distinctive trapezoid/sail shape. Companion to Crater and Hydra.

Notable Features:
• Gienah (γ Crv) - Brightest star
• Antennae Galaxies (NGC 4038/4039)
• Small but distinctive quadrilateral
• Sits on back of Hydra

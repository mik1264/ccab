Crux

Name: The Southern Cross

Abbreviation: Cru

Hemisphere: Southern

Best Seen: May

Mythology: Most famous southern constellation. Used for navigation, points toward South Celestial Pole. Smallest of all 88 constellations.

Notable Features:
• Acrux (α Cru) - Southernmost 1st magnitude star
• Mimosa (β Cru) - Blue-white giant
• Coalsack Nebula - Dark nebula nearby
• Jewel Box Cluster (NGC 4755)

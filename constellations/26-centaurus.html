Centaurus

Name: The Centaur

Abbreviation: Cen

Hemisphere: Southern

Best Seen: May

Mythology: Chiron, the wise centaur and teacher of heroes. One of the largest constellations.

Notable Features:
• Alpha Centauri - Closest star system (4.37 ly)
• Proxima Centauri - Nearest individual star
• Hadar (β Cen) - 10th brightest star
• Omega Centauri - Largest globular cluster

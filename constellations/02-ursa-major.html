Ursa Major

Name: The Great Bear

Abbreviation: UMa

Hemisphere: Northern

Best Seen: April

Mythology: In Greek mythology, Callisto was transformed into a bear by Zeus's jealous wife Hera. Zeus placed her in the sky to protect her.

Contains: The Big Dipper asterism, one of the most recognizable patterns in the night sky.

Notable Stars:
• Dubhe (α UMa)
• Merak (β UMa)
• Alioth (ε UMa)
• Mizar & Alcor (double star)

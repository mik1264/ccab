Hydra

Name: The Water Serpent

Abbreviation: Hya

Hemisphere: Equatorial

Best Seen: April

Mythology: The many-headed serpent slain by Hercules as his second labor. Longest constellation in the sky, stretching over 100 degrees.

Notable Features:
• Alphard (α Hya) - "The Solitary One"
• M48, M68, M83 - Notable deep sky objects
• Largest constellation by area
• Ghost of Jupiter Nebula (NGC 3242)

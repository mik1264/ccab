Cygnus

Name: The Swan (Northern Cross)

Abbreviation: Cyg

Best Seen: September

Mythology: Various myths including Zeus disguised as a swan. The Northern Cross asterism is visible to the naked eye.

Notable Features:
• Deneb (α Cyg) - Summer Triangle star
• Albireo (β Cyg) - Beautiful double star
• Cygnus X-1 - Black hole candidate
• Lies in rich Milky Way

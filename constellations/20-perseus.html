Perseus

Name: The Hero

Abbreviation: Per

Hemisphere: Northern

Best Seen: December

Mythology: Perseus, who slew Medusa and rescued Andromeda. Depicted holding Medusa's head (Algol).

Notable Features:
• Algol (β Per) - "Demon Star", famous eclipsing binary
• Mirfak (α Per) - Brightest star
• Double Cluster (NGC 869/884)
• Radiant of Perseid meteor shower

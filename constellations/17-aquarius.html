Aquarius

Name: The Water Bearer

Abbreviation: Aqr

Zodiac: Yes (January 20 - February 18)

Best Seen: October

Mythology: Ganymede, cup-bearer to the gods. One of the largest constellations, associated with rainy season.

Notable Features:
• Sadalsuud (β Aqr) - "Luckiest of the lucky"
• M2 - Globular cluster
• Helix Nebula (NGC 7293) - Closest planetary nebula
• Saturn Nebula (NGC 7009)

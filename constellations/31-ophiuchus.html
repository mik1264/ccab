Ophiuchus

Name: The Serpent Bearer

Abbreviation: Oph

Hemisphere: Equatorial

Best Seen: July

Mythology: Asclepius, the healer who could bring back the dead. The "13th zodiac sign" - Sun passes through it.

Notable Features:
• Rasalhague (α Oph) - "Head of the serpent collector"
• Barnard's Star - Second nearest star system
• Rich in globular clusters (M9, M10, M12, M14, M19, M62, M107)
• Divides Serpens Caput and Serpens Cauda

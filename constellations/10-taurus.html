Taurus

Name: The Bull

Abbreviation: Tau

Zodiac: Yes (April 20 - May 20)

Best Seen: January

Mythology: Zeus transformed into a white bull to abduct Europa.

Notable Features:
• Aldebaran (α Tau) - Orange giant "eye of the bull"
• Pleiades (M45) - Seven Sisters star cluster
• Hyades - Nearest open cluster
• Crab Nebula (M1) - Supernova remnant

Canis Minor

Name: The Lesser Dog

Abbreviation: CMi

Hemisphere: Northern

Best Seen: March

Mythology: Orion's smaller hunting dog. One of the smallest constellations with only two bright stars.

Notable Features:
• Procyon (α CMi) - 8th brightest star, "before the dog"
• Forms Winter Triangle with Sirius and Betelgeuse
• Gomeisa (β CMi) - Second star
• Very simple constellation pattern

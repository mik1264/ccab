Auriga

Name: The Charioteer

Abbreviation: Aur

Hemisphere: Northern

Best Seen: February

Mythology: Erichthonius, king of Athens who invented the four-horse chariot. Often depicted holding a goat (Capella) and her kids.

Notable Features:
• Capella (α Aur) - 6th brightest star, actually four stars
• M36, M37, M38 - Three bright open clusters
• Haedi - "The Kids" star cluster
• Crosses Milky Way, rich in star clusters

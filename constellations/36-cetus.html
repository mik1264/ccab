Cetus

Name: The Whale/Sea Monster

Abbreviation: Cet

Hemisphere: Equatorial

Best Seen: November

Mythology: Sea monster sent to devour Andromeda but slain by Perseus. Fourth largest constellation.

Notable Features:
• Mira (ο Cet) - Famous variable star prototype
• Diphda (β Cet) - Brightest star
• Menkar (α Cet) - Orange giant
• Tau Ceti - Nearby sun-like star (12 ly)

Canis Major

Name: The Greater Dog

Abbreviation: CMa

Hemisphere: Southern

Best Seen: February

Mythology: Orion's hunting dog. Contains the brightest star in the night sky.

Notable Features:
• Sirius (α CMa) - Brightest star (-1.46 mag)
• "Dog Star" - rises with summer heat
• M41 - Open cluster visible to naked eye
• Adhara (ε CMa) - Second brightest in constellation

Leo

Name: The Lion

Abbreviation: Leo

Hemisphere: Northern

Best Seen: April

Mythology: Represents the Nemean Lion, a creature with impenetrable hide killed by Hercules as one of his twelve labors.

Notable Stars:
• Regulus (α Leo) - "Little King"
• Denebola (β Leo) - Lion's tail
• Algieba (γ Leo) - Binary star

Features: Contains the "Sickle" asterism forming the lion's head and mane

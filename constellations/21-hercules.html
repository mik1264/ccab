Hercules

Name: The Strongman

Abbreviation: Her

Hemisphere: Northern

Best Seen: July

Mythology: The great hero Hercules, depicted kneeling with one foot on the head of Draco. Fifth largest constellation.

Notable Features:
• Keystone asterism - four main stars
• M13 - Great Globular Cluster (naked eye)
• M92 - Bright globular cluster
• Rasalgethi (α Her) - Triple star system

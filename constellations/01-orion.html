Orion

Name: The Hunter

Abbreviation: Ori

Hemisphere: Equatorial

Best Seen: January

Mythology: Orion was a legendary hunter in Greek mythology. The constellation shows him with his club raised against Taurus the Bull.

Notable Stars:
• Betelgeuse (α Ori) - Red supergiant
• Rigel (β Ori) - Blue supergiant
• Bellatrix (γ Ori) - Blue giant
• Orion's Belt (Alnitak, Alnilam, Mintaka)

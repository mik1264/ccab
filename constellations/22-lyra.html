Lyra

Name: The Lyre

Abbreviation: Lyr

Hemisphere: Northern

Best Seen: August

Mythology: The lyre of Orpheus, the legendary musician. Small but contains one of the brightest stars in the sky.

Notable Features:
• Vega (α Lyr) - 5th brightest star, part of Summer Triangle
• M57 - Ring Nebula, famous planetary nebula
• Epsilon Lyrae - "Double Double" star
• Sheliak (β Lyr) - Eclipsing binary prototype

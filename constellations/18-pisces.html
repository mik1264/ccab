Pisces

Name: The Fishes

Abbreviation: Psc

Zodiac: Yes (February 19 - March 20)

Best Seen: November

Mythology: Aphrodite and Eros transformed into fish to escape Typhon, tied together by a cord. Contains the vernal equinox point.

Notable Features:
• Alrescha (α Psc) - "The cord" between fish
• M74 - Face-on spiral galaxy
• Contains vernal equinox (First Point of Aries)
• Large but faint constellation

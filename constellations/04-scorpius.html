Scorpius

Name: The Scorpion

Abbreviation: Sco

Hemisphere: Southern

Best Seen: July

Mythology: The scorpion that killed Orion in Greek mythology. They are placed opposite in the sky so they never appear together.

Notable Stars:
• Antares (α Sco) - Red supergiant, "rival of Mars"
• Shaula (λ Sco) - Tail sting
• Contains many bright stars

Features: Distinctive curved tail, lies in rich Milky Way region

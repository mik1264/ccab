Boötes

Name: The Herdsman

Abbreviation: Boo

Hemisphere: Northern

Best Seen: June

Mythology: The herdsman who invented the plow. Follows Ursa Major around the sky, herding the bears.

Notable Features:
• Arcturus (α Boo) - 4th brightest star, red giant
• Izar (ε Boo) - Beautiful double star "Pulcherrima"
• Kite or ice cream cone shape
• Follow the arc of Big Dipper to Arcturus

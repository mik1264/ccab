Sagittarius

Name: The Archer

Abbreviation: Sgr

Zodiac: Yes (November 22 - December 21)

Best Seen: August

Mythology: Chiron the centaur, wise teacher and archer. Points toward the center of our Milky Way galaxy.

Notable Features:
• Contains galactic center & Sgr A* black hole
• Teapot asterism - famous star pattern
• M8 (Lagoon Nebula), M17 (Omega Nebula)
• Richest region for deep sky objects

Draco

Name: The Dragon

Abbreviation: Dra

Hemisphere: Northern

Best Seen: July

Mythology: Ladon, the dragon killed by Hercules. Coils around the north celestial pole. One of the 48 Ptolemaic constellations.

Notable Features:
• Thuban (α Dra) - Former pole star (3000 BC)
• Eltanin (γ Dra) - Dragon's head, brightest star
• Cat's Eye Nebula (NGC 6543)
• Circumpolar from northern latitudes

Virgo

Name: The Virgin

Abbreviation: Vir

Zodiac: Yes (August 23 - September 22)

Best Seen: May

Mythology: Associated with Demeter/Persephone, goddess of harvest. The largest constellation of the zodiac.

Notable Features:
• Spica (α Vir) - 15th brightest star in sky
• Virgo Cluster - 1,500+ galaxies
• M87 - Supermassive black hole galaxy
• M104 - Sombrero Galaxy

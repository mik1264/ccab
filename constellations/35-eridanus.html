Eridanus

Name: The River

Abbreviation: Eri

Hemisphere: Southern

Best Seen: December

Mythology: The celestial river, associated with Po River, Nile, or the river where Phaëthon fell. Sixth largest constellation.

Notable Features:
• Achernar (α Eri) - 9th brightest star, "end of the river"
• Cursa (β Eri) - Northern end of river
• Flows from near Rigel to deep south
• Epsilon Eridani - Nearby sun-like star with planets

Aquila

Name: The Eagle

Abbreviation: Aql

Best Seen: August-September

Mythology: Zeus's eagle that carried his thunderbolts. Also associated with the eagle that abducted Ganymede.

Notable Features:
• Altair (α Aql) - Summer Triangle star, 12th brightest star
• Only 16.7 light-years away
• Rapidly rotating, oblate shape

Gemini

Name: The Twins

Abbreviation: Gem

Zodiac: Yes (May 21 - June 20)

Best Seen: February

Mythology: Castor and Pollux, the twin brothers of Greek mythology. Pollux was immortal, son of Zeus, while Castor was mortal. They shared their immortality to remain together forever in the stars.

Notable Stars:
• Pollux (β Gem) - Orange giant, brighter twin
• Castor (α Gem) - Multiple star system
• Alhena (γ Gem) - White subgiant
• Contains radiant of Geminid meteor shower

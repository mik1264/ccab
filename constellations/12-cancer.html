Cancer

Name: The Crab

Abbreviation: Cnc

Zodiac: Yes (June 21 - July 22)

Best Seen: March

Mythology: The crab sent by Hera to distract Hercules during his battle with the Hydra. Though crushed by Hercules, Hera placed it in the stars.

Notable Features:
• Faintest zodiac constellation
• M44 (Beehive Cluster) - Praesepe
• M67 - Ancient open cluster
• Acubens (α Cnc) - Multiple star system

Cassiopeia

Name: The Queen

Abbreviation: Cas

Hemisphere: Northern

Best Seen: November

Shape: Distinctive "W" or "M" shape

Mythology: Cassiopeia was a vain queen of Ethiopia who boasted about her beauty. As punishment, she was placed in the sky, eternally rotating around the celestial pole.

Notable Features:
• Easy to find opposite Big Dipper
• Contains Tycho's Supernova remnant
• Part of Perseus-Cassiopeia family

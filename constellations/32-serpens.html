Serpens

Name: The Serpent

Abbreviation: Ser

Hemisphere: Equatorial

Best Seen: July

Mythology: The serpent held by Asclepius (Ophiuchus). Unique as only constellation split into two parts.

Notable Features:
• Only constellation in two parts: Serpens Caput (head) and Serpens Cauda (tail)
• Unukalhai (α Ser) - "Serpent's neck"
• M5 - Spectacular globular cluster
• M16 - Eagle Nebula with "Pillars of Creation"

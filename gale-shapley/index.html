History

Based on Gale, D. & Shapley, L.S. (1962). “College Admissions and the Stability of Marriage.” American Mathematical Monthly, 69(1): 9–15.

2012 Nobel Prize in Economics awarded to Alvin E. Roth and Lloyd S. Shapley “for the theory of stable allocations and the practice of market design.”

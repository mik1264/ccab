Men-Optimal Deferred Acceptance

The classic Gale-Shapley (1962) algorithm where men propose and women choose. This produces the unique stable matching that is simultaneously best for every man and worst for every woman among all stable matchings.

Algorithm Pseudocode 
Men-Proposing Deferred Acceptance:

1. Each free man proposes to the
   highest-ranked woman on his list
   he has not yet proposed to.

2. Each woman who receives proposals
   holds her most-preferred suitor
   and rejects the rest.

3. Rejected men become free and
   propose to their next choice.

4. Repeat until no free men remain.

Time complexity: O(n²)
Space complexity: O(n²)

Men-Optimal Stable Matching

This is the men-optimal stable matching. Every man is assigned his best possible partner across ALL stable matchings. Simultaneously, every woman receives her worst stable partner.

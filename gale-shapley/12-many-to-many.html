Many-to-Many Matching

Both sides can match with multiple partners: workers can take multiple part-time jobs, and firms can hire multiple workers. Pairwise stability prevents any unmatched worker-firm pair from mutually benefiting.

Algorithm Mode

Algorithm Pseudocode 
Many-to-Many DA (Worker-proposing):
while ∃ worker w with unused quota
  and remaining acceptable firms:
    f ← w's top remaining firm
    if f has unused quota:
      add match (w, f)
    else if f prefers w to its
      worst current match w':
      remove (w', f), add (w, f)
    else: f rejects w

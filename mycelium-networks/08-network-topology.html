Fungal Network Topology

Mycelium networks exhibit scale-free properties: a few highly-connected hub nodes and many peripheral nodes. Compare this to random and small-world networks. Hub removal (simulating tree death) can fragment the network, while scale-free networks are resilient to random failure but vulnerable to targeted attack on hubs.

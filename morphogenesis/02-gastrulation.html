Gastrulation

Stage: Blastula
Watch as the single-layered blastula transforms into a three-layered gastrula. Cells migrate inward through the blastopore to form ectoderm (blue), mesoderm (green), and endoderm (red) - the three germ layers that give rise to all tissues.

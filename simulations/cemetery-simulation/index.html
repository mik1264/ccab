About This Simulation

Bonaventure Cemetery is one of America's most beautiful Victorian-era garden cemeteries, located on a scenic bluff of the Wilmington River in Savannah, Georgia. Established in 1846 on the grounds of the former Bonaventure Plantation, it was made famous by the book "Midnight in the Garden of Good and Evil."

This simulation models historically accurate data including:

  • 160 acres with 30,000+ burials across 17 sections
  • Victorian-era mortality patterns (high infant mortality, epidemics)
  • Historical events: Yellow Fever outbreaks, Civil War, Spanish Flu, COVID-19
  • Savannah population growth from 15,000 (1850) to 147,000 (2020)
  • Section expansions: Jewish Section (1909), American Legion Field (1945)
  • Monument styles: Victorian Gothic, Classical Roman, Egyptian Revival, Art Deco

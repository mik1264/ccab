Brian's Brain

A three-state cellular automaton that creates beautiful, ever-moving patterns. Each cell can be Off (black), On (white), or Dying (blue). Simple rules create complex behaviors: Off cells turn On if exactly 2 neighbors are On, On cells always become Dying, and Dying cells always become Off. Click to toggle cells!

About Brian's Brain

  • States: Three states create rich dynamics
    • Off (black): Inactive cell
    • On (white): Active cell
    • Dying (blue): Transitioning cell
  • Rules:
    • Off cell with exactly 2 On neighbors → On
    • On cell → Dying (always)
    • Dying cell → Off (always)
  • Neighborhood: Moore neighborhood (8 surrounding cells)
  • Properties: Creates perpetual motion, never stabilizes like Conway's Game of Life
  • Patterns: Supports gliders, guns, and other interesting structures
  • Interactive: Click to activate cells and watch them propagate
